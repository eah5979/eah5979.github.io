Welcome to New York City!

Home to the city that never sleeps!

Right next to Long Island and across the bridge, is the wonderful New York City! Undoubtedly, New York City is known for its grand skyline, food and of course Times Square. Starting from the center of Long Island, your'e about 45 miles from Manhattan, but travel time can range from 1 hour to 2 and a half hours depending on traffic.

Things to do in NYC:

  • See a broadway show
  • Try a world famous restraunt like the Stardust Diner or Carmine's
  • Visit Times Square
  • Visit SoHo and the West Village
