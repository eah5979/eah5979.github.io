Welcome to Nassau County!

Home to awesome beaches and a vibrant suburban lifestyle!

One thing Nassau county is famous for is Jones Beach. A large state park that has a boardwalk and plenty to do. It is also the home of the Jones Beach Air Show, which takes place every memorial day and usually hosts the U.S. Navy Blue Angels or the U.S. Air Force Thunderbirds.
